# 📊 Executive-Financial-Dashboard

## 📈 Executive Financial Dashboard | Power BI

An end-to-end **Business Intelligence** project that transforms raw financial sales data into interactive executive-level insights using **Power BI, Power Query, DAX, and Excel**.

**Power BI • DAX • Power Query • Excel • Business Intelligence**

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The **Executive Financial Dashboard** is designed to help business stakeholders monitor key financial metrics and evaluate business performance through an intuitive, interactive dashboard.

The project analyzes sales transactions across multiple products, customer segments, countries, and time periods to uncover trends in revenue, profitability, product performance, and operational efficiency.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

* Clean and transform raw financial data using Power Query.
* Build a star-schema data model.
* Create DAX measures for KPIs.
* Analyze Sales, Profit, Units Sold, and COGS.
* Compare product and segment performance.
* Monitor monthly sales and profit trends.
* Design an interactive executive dashboard.
* Generate actionable business insights for decision-making.

# 🔍 Key Insights

📈 Generated **$118.73M** in Total Sales.

💰 Achieved **$16.89M** in Total Profit.

📦 Sold over **1.13M Units** across all products.

🌍 Sales were distributed across multiple international markets.

🏆 **Paseo** emerged as the top-performing product in terms of revenue and profit.

📊 Government and Small Business segments contributed the highest share of sales.

📅 Sales performance varied across months, highlighting seasonal business trends.
